Chai Tea Latte Recipe

Description
This milky chai tea latte is really similar to the brand Oregon Chai.
Simply delicious either hot or iced! This will save you more money than buying the chai.
In India, each family has their own way of making it, so you should experiment to find your favorite blend.
Enjoy!
Prep Time: Total Time: Servings:
5 minutes 5 minutes 1 servings

Ingredients

You likely already have all the ingredients you'll need for this Special Chai.
Here's what you need:

  • ¾ cup boiling water.
  • Your favourite brand Tea-leaves .
  • 1 ½ teaspoons honey.
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar.
  • ¾ cup milk.

Directions

It's really simple:

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Pour boiling water over chai tea bag in a mug; let steep 4 to 6 minutes. Remove and discard tea bag.
  3. Stir honey and sugar into tea to dissolve. Stir milk through tea to serve.

Sip it with Biscuits or chips and enjoy!
