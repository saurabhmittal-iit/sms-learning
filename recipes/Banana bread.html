Banana Bread Recipe

Description
This is a simple recipe for banana bread that is easy to make and tastes delicious. It is perfect for breakfast or as a snack.
The bread is moist and flavorful, with a hint of cinnamon and nutmeg. You can add nuts or chocolate chips if you like, or leave them out for a classic banana bread.
Enjoy!

Ingredients

You likely already have all the ingredients you'll need for this banana bread recipe on hand. If not, here's what to add to your grocery list:

  • Flour: All-purpose flour gives the banana bread structure.
  • Baking soda: Baking soda acts as a leavener. This helps the bread rise.
  • Salt: A pinch of salt enhances the overall flavor, but it won't make the loaf taste salty.
  • Butter: A stick of butter lends richness, moisture, and irresistible flavor.
  • Sugar: Granulated sugar sweetens the bread, and adds a hint of warmth.
  • Eggs: Eggs bind the ingredients together and help the bread rise.
  • Bananas: Overripe bananas are the star of the show. They add natural sweetness and moisture.

Steps to Make Banana Bread

Here's how to make banana bread from scratch:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F and grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the mashed bananas.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and bake for 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Let the bread cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then transfer it to a wire rack to cool completely.

    8. Slice and serve the banana bread, and enjoy!
